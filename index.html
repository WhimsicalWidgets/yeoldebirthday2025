Hear Ye! Hear Ye! Diana, Brad and Kelly's 400th Birthday Celebration

Join us for a weekend of merriment, feasting, and Renaissance revelry in the fair lands of Gilroy!

When

October 17-19, 2025

Where

Gilroy, California

Cost

$300-$500 pp* sliding scale

*Amanda (and anyone able) to cover extra cost

Royal Schedule

Friday, October 17 - Chill & Explore Day

Morning/Afternoon

  • Arrive in Gilroy and settle into your Airbnb
  • Downtown Gilroy stroll: Explore the Historic Paseo, grab coffee, pop into Ashford's Heirlooms or Garbo's Antiques, and visit the Gilroy Museum
  • Optional: Visit a local fruit stand or Garlic World for snacks or ingredients

Afternoon/Evening

  • Possible wine tasting at Solis Winery, Sarah's Vineyard, or Satori Cellars
  • Dinner out at Mamma Mia's (Mediterranean) or Westside Grill (casual steakhouse)

Evening

  • Games and Crafting activities to prep for Ren Fair

Saturday, October 18 - Renaissance Fair Day

Day

  • Spend the full day at the Northern California Renaissance Faire in Hollister
  • Dress up, enjoy the jousts, mead, live shows, and artisan market

Evening

  • Return to Airbnb for a private chef-prepared dinner
  • Themed birthday dinner prepared for our birthday darlings with music, treats, and custom cocktails
  • End with a fireplace moment, s'mores, and stargazing

Sunday, October 19 - Birthday Celebration

Morning

  • Optional horseback riding at Mt. Madonna Stables (book ahead)
  • Alt activity- hike or picnic at Mt. Madonna Park or Coyote Lake

Afternoon

  • Winery tasting around 1–2 PM
  • Options include Clos LaChance, Fortino, or Kirigin Cellars, all with beautiful settings and relaxed vineyard atmospheres

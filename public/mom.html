To My Dearest Mother

With all my love

Dearest Mom,

You are the sweetest, funniest, most wonderful mother in the world. You are my darling, my confidante, my greatest supporter. My love for you stretches across all the world and back again.

Every day I wish for your happiness and health, and I promise to do everything in my power to bring you joy, comfort, and prosperity. Your wellbeing means more to me than anything.

Thank you for caring for me in countless ways - for your patience when I was learning, your wisdom when I was lost, your comfort when I was hurt, and your laughter that brightens even my darkest days.

I'm so incredibly grateful that you're here to share this life with me - to laugh together, to love together, and to create beautiful memories that I'll cherish forever.

With all my heart,
Your loving boy child
